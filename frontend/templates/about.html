About the CredAI System

An end-to-end overview of the objectives, timeline, machine learning pipeline, and architecture.

Problem Statement

Manual credit card verification is slow, expensive, and susceptible to cognitive bias. Traditional automated credit check processes frequently operate as "black boxes," leaving candidates without actionable suggestions for improvement when their applications are declined.

CredAI solves this by serving transparent, explainable predictions in real-time, matching high accuracy scoring models with regulatory compliance standards.

Development & Pipeline Timeline

1

Data Engineering & Cleaning

Demographics dataset and repayment history details are loaded, cleaned of duplicate rows, and occupations are standardized. Outliers are filtered and records are mapped.

2

Target Variable Creation

Repayment timelines are aggregated. Clients with over 30 days overdue payments are classified as class 0 (Rejected), and on-time payees as class 1 (Approved).

3

Feature Engineering & Preprocessing

We derive income per family member and age bins, fit standard scalar and one-hot encoders, and save serialization pipelines to joblib files.

4

Model Tuning & Champion Selection

We train and hyper-tune 8 classifiers. Models are cross-validated and compared on precision, recall, and F1 metrics to select the best production weights.

5

Flask Server & API serving

API endpoints process JSON requests, validate bounds, execute model predictions in-memory, and audit log histories to SQLite tables.
